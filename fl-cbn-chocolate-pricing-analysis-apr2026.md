## Market Insights

### CBN Market Gap

**Finding:** Florida has LIMITED 1:1:1 THC:CBD:CBN chocolate options

**Available Ratios:**
- ✅ THC-only chocolate (abundant)
- ✅ 1:1 THC:CBD chocolate (common)
- ✅ 1:1 THC:CBN chocolate (emerging)
- ⚠️ 1:1:1 THC:CBD:CBN chocolate (**RARE**)

**Opportunity:**
- Most 1:1:1 products are gummies/chews, not chocolate
- Premium chocolate format with triple-ratio = **MARKET WHITESPACE**

### Price Elasticity

**$10-15:** Budget-conscious, high volume potential  
**$15-25:** Sweet spot for most operators  
**$25-35:** Premium positioning, specialty formulations  

**CBN Premium:** Products with CBN command 10-30% price premium over THC-only

### Format Preferences

**Chocolate Bars** (most common):
- 10-20 piece bars
- Easy dosing (10mg per piece)
- Resealable packaging

**Chocolate Cubez** (The Flowery):
- Individual pieces
- Novel format
- Budget-friendly

**Gummies** (CBN ratios):
- More CBN options in gummy format
- Easier manufacturing for multi-ratio
- Fruit flavors mask cannabis taste
